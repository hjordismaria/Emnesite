Spaghetti Meatballs

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 400g minced beef
  • 3-4 slices white bread
  • 3/4 dl water
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 dl fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 dl parmesan cheese, grated

Sauce

  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 paprikas
  • 1 can chopped tomatos
  • 1 can tomato passata
  • 2 tsp. basilika
  • salt and pepper

Steps

Meatballs

  1. Warm the oven at 180Â°C, fan
  2. Cut the crust of the bread and tear the bread into a bowl. Pour the water over it and let it stand for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Shape into medium-sized meatballs and place on a baking sheet
  5. Put the meatballs into the oven for 20 minutes.

Sauce

  1. Chop the onion, garlic and paprika's and cook with some olive oil in a large pan or a pot until soft.
  2. Add the tomato passata and chopped tomatoes and season with basil, salt and pepper and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  3. When the meatballs are ready, add them to the sauce
  4. Serve with Spaghetti
