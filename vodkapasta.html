Vodka Pasta

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta, fx. Rigatoni or penne
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 1 onion,finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 dl vodka
  • 100g tomato purée
  • 250 ml crean
  • 30g parmesan cheese
  • salt, pepper and chili flakes
  • fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and cook your pasta.
  2. In a pan cook the onion and garlic with the butter until soft.
  3. Add the tomato purée, salt, pepper and chilli flakes, you want to cook the tomato purée until it becomes dark red.
  4. Add the vodka and cook for about a minute.
  5. Next add the cream and let it simmer for about 5 minutes until it turns creamier.
  6. Then add the boiled pasta and parmesan cheese and blend it all together.
  7. Top with fresh parsley and serve
