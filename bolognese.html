Alfredo

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 tsp. basil
  • 1 tsp. oregano
  • 1/2 tsp. smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 500g tagliatelle
  • 500 ml cream
  • 90g parmesan cheese
  • 3 tbsp. butter
  • 2 shallots, chopped
  • 4 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 dl fresh parsley, chopped
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces, place in a bowl and season with salt
  2. Add the pepper, basil, oregano, smoked paprika, garlic powder and 2 tbsp. of olive oil and mix it together until spread evenly
  3. Chop the shallots and grate the parmesan cheese and set both aside into seperate bowls.
  4. Heat 2 tbsp. of olive oil in a large pan. Cook the chicken on medium heat until cooked through
  5. Set the chicken aside and in the same pan cook the shallots with the butter until soft.
  6. Add the cream and then the parmesan cheese.
  7. When the sauce has thickened and the parmesan has melted, taste and add salt if necessary.
  8. Keep warm over low heat.
  9. Boil water in a pot with salt and olive oil.
  10. Add the pasta and cook until al dente.
  11. When the pasta is ready add it to the sauce along with the chicken.
  12. Heat the pan over medium heat until the sauce thickens and everything is mixed well.
  13. Top with fresh parsley and serve.
